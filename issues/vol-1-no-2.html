Merchant Success · Above the Fold

Sierra Madre Goes Live; First Sale Lands at Golf Galaxy

A clean cutover, an unhyped retail debut, and a "safety stock done the right way" story make Seth's Wednesday call the week's most fun lead — even as a wholesale oversell scare at Oliver Cabell tested the team's incident discipline.
By the Merchant Success Desk · Filed from #merchant-success

"Sierra Madre is live on GoodDay!" Seth Horton declared in #merchant-success on Wednesday afternoon, capping a working session with Sierra Madre's Bonny. The brand is officially moving onto GoodDay as their inventory source of truth — Shopify now pulling levels directly, cycle count adjustments pushed through together on the call.

The retail headline lands inside the launch story: Sierra Madre had shipped to ten Golf Galaxy stores for testing and started selling the prior weekend. Six units moved in the first few days with zero hype and zero marketing behind it. Golf Galaxy is already asking about restocks and spring orders.

The technical execution was clean. A full apparel cycle count reconciled deltas between GoodDay and Shopify. Legacy sample-sale SKUs were zeroed out. Accessories — high quantities, low risk — are pulling from current Shopify levels for now. Bonny wanted a buffer without faking numbers, so the team moved 1–2 units per SKU into Reserved in GoodDay rather than masking truth in Shopify. GoodDay is the truth; available is what flows to the storefront.

The week wasn't without alarm. By Monday afternoon, James Hargett was pushing Eng to validate a Ser investigation suggesting Oliver Cabell may have "drastically oversold wholesale by thousands of units." After four threads and a Tuesday morning post-mortem, James walked it back: OC had duplicated orders rather than blocks (likely a user-error pattern) and only one SKU on those duplicated SOs actually oversold — and was covered with a transfer. Wardell's directive to Tater and Harish: "sit down in the morning together and write out EXACTLY what happened without any AI involved because these threads are impossible to follow."

Next up: pulling Sierra Madre's open draft wholesale orders into GoodDay, allocating, and pushing through the wholesale channel. ShipStation integration is on the horizon when they're ready to move off Shopify-printed labels.
